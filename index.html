About the Conference

What is ICIDHAI?

ICIDHAI 2026 aims to critically examine the transformative impact of artificial intelligence on language, literature, and cultural studies. It provides a global platform for scholars, researchers, and practitioners to engage in interdisciplinary dialogue bridging humanities and computational sciences.

The conference explores how artificial intelligence is reshaping literary and linguistic studies while fostering interdisciplinary collaboration between scholars in the humanities and computational fields.

Why Attend?

  • Present your research to an international audience
  • Network with scholars from 8 countries
  • Publish your work in prestigious journals or ISBN books
  • Explore cutting-edge AI applications in humanities
